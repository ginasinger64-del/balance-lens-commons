Self-funded enforcement oscillates because success destroys its own funding source. As defectors decline, confiscated payoff declines. Enforcement weakens. Defectors recover. Funding returns. The two populations become dynamically coupled — in this model architecture, revenue is proportional to remaining defection.

Toy model — a minimal recent-payoff replicator on a logistically regenerating resource (α=2, g=δ=0.20, κ=2, 5-step memory). Live equations, not a recording. The ordering shown here survives finite-N stochastic and spatial versions; specific numbers are model-dependent and carry no claim about real systems. Tip: slide the enforcement cost up under Unfunded to watch the collapse threshold, then flip to Self-funded at the same cost.
